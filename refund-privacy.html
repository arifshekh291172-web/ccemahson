Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At CCE Mahson, we believe in transparency and fairness. This Refund Policy explains when a refund may or may not be applicable.

1. Accidental / Wrong Payment

If a payment is made accidentally (for example, wrong amount, duplicate transaction, or technical error), the student is eligible for a full refund.

To be eligible, the refund request must be raised within 24 hours of the transaction with valid proof.

2. Multiple Accidental Payments

If accidental payments occur multiple times, refunds will be provided for up to three (3) accidental transactions.

Repeated misuse beyond this limit may result in rejection of further refund requests.

3. Course Change of Mind

If a student enrolls in a course and later decides not to continue due to a change of mind or personal preference, no refund will be provided.

Students are advised to carefully review course details, duration, and fees before making a payment.

4. Course Access & Usage

Once course access, study material, or certification process has started, the payment becomes non-refundable.

5. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method.
